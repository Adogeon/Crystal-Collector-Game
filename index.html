Crystal Collector

The rule

  1. You will be give a random number at the start of the game.
  2. Your goals is to match that number using the value of the four crystals. You will fail if your score go higher.
  3. Each crystals have a hidden value. You will have to click on them to reveal their value.
  4. The values will reset themself each round.
